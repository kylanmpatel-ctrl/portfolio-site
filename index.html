01 — About me

I'm a student who builds things instead of just reading about them.

I'm a Junior at Saint Mary's High School. I got interested in business and finance when I realized that understanding how money, markets, and organizations work is the foundation of almost everything — and I wanted to understand it for myself, not just read about it.

I don't have years of experience — but I have genuine curiosity, the ability to learn fast, and a habit of finishing what I start. This portfolio is proof of that.

Outside of school, I'm a big sports guy — I love watching and playing golf, basketball, and baseball, usually with my family. Sports taught me a lot about competition, staying composed under pressure, and what it actually takes to improve at something over time.
